About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sweet Crumb started as a small family kitchen experiment. Today, we are proud to serve the community with organic ingredients and traditional baking methods. Every loaf tells a story of passion and dedication.


We believe that bread is the heart of every home. That's why we wake up at 4 AM every day to ensure you get the freshest bake possible.
