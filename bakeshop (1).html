About Us

A Tradition of Taste

Founded in 2015, Sweet Crumb started as a small family kitchen experiment. Today, we are proud to serve our community with organic ingredients and time-honored recipes.


We believe that bread is the heart of every home. That's why we never use preservatives—just flour, water, yeast, salt, and a whole lot of passion.
